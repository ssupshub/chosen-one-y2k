— THE FIRST SCROLL: OF THE CHOSEN —
One who surfs the web at dusk,
Who hears the modem sing and husk,
Shall click the link and save us all,
Before the great hard drive shall fall.
Their IP address shall glow with light,
Their ping shall be low, their packets bright,
And MALKOR.EXE shall fear their name,
For the Chosen One has entered the game.

— THE SECOND SCROLL: OF MALKOR —
From a floppy disk in desert lands,
He rose with corrupted, viral hands,
MALKOR.EXE, the Virus King,
Whose popup ads cause suffering.
His weakness lies in one true thing —
The Sacred Zip, what fortune brings,
When unpacked with the Password of Ages,
He shall be banished from all pages.

— Verse 1-8, Scroll II, Netscape Codex

— THE THIRD SCROLL: OF VICTORY —
When seven kingdoms stand as one,
And all the crystal defrags done,
The Ancient Homepage glows with white,
And dial-up speeds shall reach full light.
The Chosen signs the guestbook last,
And so the age of darkness passed,
The internet reborn and bright,
56k burns through the night.
